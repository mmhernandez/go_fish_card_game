The goal of the game is to find a matching face value pair (two 4's or 2 Jacks, etc.) for each card in your hand.
To complete your turn, start by laying down any pairs of cards in your hand by highlighting them and clicking the 'Lay Down Pairs' button. Next, ask the computer for a card to see if they can help you make another pair. Your turn ends when the computer does not have the card in question.
